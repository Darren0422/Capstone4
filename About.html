Who are we?

Welcome to WeeboUnion, your ultimate destination for all things anime, manga, and otaku culture! Here, we are passionate fans who love to immerse ourselves in the captivating world of Japanese animation, manga, and everything related to our otaku hobbies.

At WeeboUnion, our website serves as a dedicated space where we pour our thoughts and share our experiences with fellow enthusiasts. You'll find a diverse range of content to satisfy your cravings, including insightful episode commentaries on currently airing shows, in-depth reviews of anime and manga that we have finished, as well as engaging editorials and personal posts that explore various aspects of otaku culture.

As ordinary individuals with an extraordinary love for anime, manga, and all things related, we hold a deep aspiration to one day visit Japan and immerse ourselves in the vibrant and exciting otaku culture firsthand. Our shared dream fuels our dedication to creating a community where we can come together to celebrate and indulge in our passion.

At WeeboUnion, we are genuine and devoted fans of All Things Japan. From the fascinating people to the delectable food, the intricate language to the rich cultural heritage, and, of course, the awe-inspiring animation, there is simply nothing quite like anime. It is this profound appreciation that motivated us to begin writing reviews of the anime series we watch, enabling us to share our enthusiasm and love for the medium with fellow enthusiasts like you.

We wholeheartedly appreciate your visit and hope that WeeboUnion becomes your go-to resource for all your anime-related cravings. So, irasshai! Thank you for stopping by, and we invite you to embark on an exhilarating journey through the captivating world of anime and manga with us. Enjoy your exploration of WeeboUnion and have a fantastic time connecting with fellow fans who share your passion!

It's all about passion and people

- WeeboUnion creator Darren
